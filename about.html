About Us

At Samrades, we are a Thanjavur-based IT company dedicated to empowering businesses through innovative digital solutions. Our core services include website development, custom software development, secure hosting, IT infrastructure services, and IT consulting. We prioritize quality, scalability, and user-centric design.

Founded by a passionate tech enthusiast, Samrades bridges the gap between local businesses and cutting-edge solutions. We’re committed to delivering reliable, future-ready systems to help you thrive in a digital world.

Our Founder

Samrades was established by a visionary entrepreneur from Thanjavur who is passionate about delivering world-class IT services to local and global businesses.

With a background in system architecture, software development, and consulting, our founder emphasizes innovation and long-term client trust.

A mentor and community builder, our founder inspires the team to continually grow, innovate, and make a difference.
