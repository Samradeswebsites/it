About Us

At Samrades, we are a Thanjavur-based IT company dedicated to empowering businesses through innovative digital solutions. Our core services include website development, custom software development, secure hosting solutions, IT infrastructure services, and expert IT consulting tailored to your business needs. With a strong focus on quality, scalability, and user experience, we deliver technology that drives growth and builds digital resilience.

Founded by a passionate technology enthusiast with a vision to bridge the gap between local businesses and cutting-edge IT solutions, Samrades combines technical expertise with personalized service. Our team is committed to delivering reliable, future-ready solutions that help our clients stay ahead in a rapidly evolving digital world.

Our Founder

Samrades was founded by a visionary entrepreneur from Thanjavur with a passion for bridging the gap between local businesses and world-class digital solutions. With a strong technical background and deep understanding of market challenges, our founder envisioned a future where even the smallest enterprise could access scalable and secure IT services tailored to their needs.

With years of experience in software development, system architecture, and consulting, our founder has laid the foundation for a company that values innovation, simplicity, and long-term client relationships. Their leadership is marked by a commitment to ethical technology, user-centered design, and empowering local talent to create globally relevant solutions.

Beyond business, our founder is a mentor, a community advocate, and a firm believer in lifelong learning — inspiring the Samrades team to continuously grow, innovate, and serve with purpose.
